B-vitamins in optimal form and dosing

Optimum contains the biologically active (co-enzyme) forms the body uses directly: B2 as riboflavin-5′-phosphate, B6 as pyridoxal-5′-phosphate (P-5-P, involved in 140+ enzyme reactions), and B12 as methylcobalamine (12.5 µg, 500% RDA) — all more bioavailable than the cheaper synthetic forms.

It also contains extra vitamin B1, because the body needs more of it: B1 is essential for burning carbohydrates and turning glucose into energy, which matters most for the brain (about 2% of body weight but ~20% of energy use).

Good to know & warnings

Read this information before using Multivitamin Optimum.

  • Keep out of reach of young children.
  • Store cool, dry and sealed.
  • Protect from heat, light and moisture.
  • Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.
  • A food supplement is not a substitute for a varied, balanced diet and a healthy lifestyle.
  • Not suitable for children aged 17 and under.
  • If you use coumarin derivatives (anticoagulants such as warfarin), consult an expert before taking a vitamin K2 supplement.
